Amplify Team

Mission Statement

“Initiating an ongoing process to enhance the visibility of the department's talented faculty and students by refining the external presentation across platforms, including social media and the website, to better reflect our identity and impact on the world.”


Amplify team student internship is a paid opportunity aimed at fostering stronger connections between the department and the student body, including student-led organizations. The interns work directly with the departmental Committee to develop creative and impactful content that enhances visibility and engagement across the UC San Diego Music website and social media platforms.


One of the primary objectives of the Amplify Team is the creation of media content—such as video interviews, documentation of creative processes, and resource-sharing—to spotlight the vibrant work of our students, faculty, and community.

Student Organizations

Student organizations are a good place to find on-campus performance opportunities and socialize with musicians and artists on campus. We recommend checking the orgs out especially for new students looking for a community. Discord is usually a good place to ask questions or communicate with people, yet communication platforms vary by organization.
